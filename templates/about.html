About Fundamental Design

Fundamental Design is a leading civil engineering and contracting company based in Muranga, Kenya. With decades of experience, we deliver innovative, sustainable, and high-quality solutions for infrastructure, construction, and industrial projects across the nation.

  • Locally owned and operated
  • Multi-disciplinary team of experts
  • ISO certified and award-winning
  • Trusted by public and private sector clients

Our Mission

To build a better Kenya through engineering excellence, innovation, and integrity.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected civil engineering company in Kenya and beyond.

Our Values

  • Quality
  • Safety
  • Teamwork
  • Community
  • Sustainability

Our History

Founded in 2009 in Muranga, Fundamental Design began as a small team of passionate engineers. Over the years, we have grown into a nationally recognized company, delivering landmark projects and setting new standards in the industry.

  • 2009: Company founded in Muranga
  • 2012: First major road project completed
  • 2016: Expanded to mechanical and electrical works
  • 2020: ISO certification and national awards
  • 2023: Over 120 projects completed
